Project Details :

MENTOR: Rajasekhar Dondapati (PhD-IIT Kharagpur)

NAME OF THE PROJECT:EXPERIMENTAL INVESTIGATION OF UNSTEADY STATE HEAT TRANSFER IN CONDENSOR WITH MIXED REFRIGERANTS WITH MIXED REFRIGERANTS R290 AND R600a

Description : It was reported in the literature that the use of mixed refrigerants in refrigeration systems can enhance the performance of the refrigerators. Hence, in the present work, two refrigerants are chosen (R-290 and R-600a) to evaluate the performance of domestic refrigerators. The refrigeration cycle consists of four major processes. The isentropic compression in the compressor, isobaric heat rejection in the condensor, isenthalpic reduction in pressure and isobaric heat addition in the evaporator. The present work aims at investigation of unsteady state heat transfer in the condensor with mixed refrigerants (R-290 and R-600a). The reduction in temperature of the refrigerant with time is experimentally investigated.
